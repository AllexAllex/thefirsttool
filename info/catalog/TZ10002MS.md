## TZ10002MS Шестиосевой фрезерно-сверлильный станок

![TZ10002MS](TZ10002MS.webp)


Mini Metal Six Shafts Drilling And Milling Machine TZ10002MS

Feature:
1. Motor case and headstock are jointed. Maximum motor power is 144W by using more powerful motor. Some parts are electroplated to make the machine more beautiful and practical.
2. Main parts: headstock, radial arm, long machine bed, short machine bed, big slider, small slider, motor blade, connection piece, dividing positioner, gear etc. All of the parts are made of metal. Function: drilling, milling, processing by dividing positioner.
3. By using dividing plate, we can drill the round workpiece equally.
4.There are three circles of holes on the dividing plate and the number of the holes respectively is 36,40,48. We can choose them as needed.
Technical parameters:  
1.Motor speed :12,000rpm/min
2.Input voltage/current/power:12VDC/5A/60W , Certificate transformer passed: 3C
3.Axis Y travel :32mm, Axis X,Z travel :145mm
4.Working material: wood, plastic, acrylic ,soft metal( gold, silver, copper, aluminum etc.),stone, jewel etc.
