## Z4000 4 базовых пластиковых станков

![Z4000](Z4000.webp)

4 In 1 basic mini machine kit Z4000

Features: The four-in-one set can form four kinds of machine tools with different functions (wire sawing machine, wood lathe, Sand Mill and hand-held machine tool), and only one of them can be assembled and used at a time. 
1. Motor speed: 20000 revolutions per minute. 
2. Input voltage/current/power: DC12VDC/2A/24 W, the input voltage of switching power AC110V-240V. It is a good tool for family and school children to make aerial models and other woodworking models
