## TZ20005MA Фрезерный станок с расточной фрезой, модификация "А"

![TZ20005MA](TZ20005MA.webp)

Big Power Mini Metal Milling Machine A TZ20005MA

 Feature:
1. Motor case and headstock are jointed. Maximum motor power is 144W by using more powerful motor. Some parts are electroplated to make the machine more beautiful and practical.
2.Main parts: headstock, long machine bed, short machine bed ,big slider, small slider, motor blade, connection piece, vice ,gear etc. All of the parts above are made of metal.  
Technical parameters:  
1.Motor speed :12,000rpm/min
2.Input voltage/current/power:12VDC/5A/60W
3、  Axis Y /Z travel :32mm， Axis X travel:145mm；
4、Blade panel: it can clamp 4mm*4mm blade tools  
5..Maximum working material diameter clamped by vice:50mm 6.With 0.02mm scale line ,the handwheel increases the precision when processing.
7,Motor power :144W。
8.Between slider space ,there is plastic elastic material to adjust the precision.
9. Working material: wood, plastic, acrylic, soft metal(gold, silver, copper,  aluminum etc.)
10. It is dangerous as the blade tool spin very fast and people have to be guided by the professional when using it
