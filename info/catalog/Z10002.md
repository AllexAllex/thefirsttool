## Z10002 Сверлильный станок с поворотной осью и делительным устройством (УДГ)

![Z10002](Z10002.webp)

Drilling Machine With Dividing Plate Z10002

 Feature:
1. By using dividing plate, we can drill the round workpiece equally.
 2. There are three circles of the holes on the dividing plate and the number of the holes respectively is 36,40,48. We can choose them as needed.
3.Machine color is made up of red and black.
Technical parameters:
1.Motor speed :20,000rpm/min, motor blade and gear are made of metal.  
2.Input voltage/current/power:12VDC/2A/24W
3.Working material: wood, engineer plastic, soft metal(aluminum ,copper etc.)
4. Maximum workpiece diameter clamped by three-jaw chuck:50mm
5.The transformer has over-current protection, over-voltage protection, over heating protection
