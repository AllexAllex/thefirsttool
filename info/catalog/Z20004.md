## Z20004 Сверлильный станок

![Z20004](Z20004.webp)

Drilling Machine Z20004

 Feature:
1.Working material: wood board, plywood, soft meta(aluminum ,copper etc.),engineering plastic. Machine color is made up of red and black. Easy operation, it can be used to drill and grind by hand, and it can drill holes with equal ratio on the workpiece within 180 degrees by using dividing plate.
Technical parameter:
1.Motor speed :20,000rpm/min, motor and gear are made of metal
2.Input voltage/current/power:12VDC/2A/24W
3.Slider travel:30mm，50mm
4.Collets：1-6mm。 5.Working table size：123*100mm. 6.The transformer has over-current protection, over-voltage protection, over heating protection.
